Parts of A Clock

Clock Dials

Clock dials are typically made using a meticulous process involving skilled craftsmanship and modern technology. Initially, a base material like brass or steel is selected and shaped into the desired size and form. Intricate designs and numerals are then either engraved or printed onto the surface, often with precision machinery. Finally, the dial may undergo finishing touches such as painting, gilding, or coating to achieve the desired aesthetic and durability.
